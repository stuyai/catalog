Mission:
BSL aims to create a friendly and welcoming environment where people of color can freely indulge in important and productive race-related discussions.

Meeting Schedule:
Meetings are hosted weekly on Wednesdays, but special events such as Talk Circles and speaker events may be held in accordance with student availability.
