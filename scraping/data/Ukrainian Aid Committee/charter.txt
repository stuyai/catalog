Therefore, we hope this committee not only directly provides aid to Ukraine, but also provides a sense of peace and unity within those willing to stand up for the suffering in Ukraine.

The treasurer will be responsible for counting the donations, whether they are in money form or necessities such as clothing. Treasurer and social media manager positions will be re-chosen each year, although the same person could be re-elected. Leaders are expected to remain respectful snd motivated, if a member of the club suggests a leader be impeached, the club will hold a vote based on wether or not that leader should be removed.
