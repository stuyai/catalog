We will discuss well-known court cases and bring notable/influential guest speakers to give us an insight into various types of law. As a group, we will talk about gender inequality to empower women to raise their voices and help each other grow as individuals. In general, this club will provide leadership opportunities, motivate students to pursue law, and help students work together. Fundamentally speaking, stuy women in law will also prepare students for the future and give them a glimpse of law and how it plays into society. Presidents are in charge of maintaining the club and creating informational slides, while directors will bring guest speakers, organize trips, and promote the club. On the contrary, the chair is to keep the presidents and members in check.
