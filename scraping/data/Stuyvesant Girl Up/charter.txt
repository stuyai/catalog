In order to foster a democracy in which everyone is represented, we will use a popular vote system to impeach any current leaders of the club or elect a member of the club to the board. If someone proposes to impeach anyone in a leadership position, they will be required to provide a logical explanation for why they think the officer deserves to be removed from office.
